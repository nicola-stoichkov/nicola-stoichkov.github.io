Who I am

Background & Skills

I'm a Finance graduate currently pursuing a Master's in International Project Management at UNWE, while working as a Data Operations Intern at Momentum Data - a startup offering ABM marketing through LinkedIn automation.

My work sits at the intersection of data quality, campaign analytics, and process efficiency. I enjoy finding the friction in a workflow and removing it - whether that's enriching a prospect list, maintaining dashboards, or designing an AI-assisted automation that reduced a repetitive weekly task by ~40%, complete with workflow documentation turned into a reusable Skills file.

An early adopter actively applying AI tools to real operational problems - from prompt engineering to building documented, team-ready workflows.

Originally from Bulgaria, with experience studying in Rome on Erasmus+ and a published research paper in a peer-reviewed economics journal. Looking for data-focused opportunities in international environments.
